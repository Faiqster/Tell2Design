The balcony is at the north of the building, and adjacent to the master room and common room 2. It measures about 23ft wide and 2ft long. Bathroom 1 is at the west of the building, to the south of the master room. It measures about 6ft wide and 3ft long. Bathroom 2 is at the west of the building, adjacent and to the south of bathroom 1. It measures about 6ft wide and 3ft long. Common room 1 is at the south-west part of the building, to the west of the kitchen, and south of the living room. It measures about 12ft wide and 11ft long. Common room 2 is at the north-east part of the building, to the north of the living room, and behind the balcony. It measures about 11ft wide and 14ft long. The kitchen is at the south-east part of the building, to the east of common room 1, and the south of the living room. It measures about 10 ft wide and 3 ft long. The living room is at the center of the building and lies between the kitchen, common room 1, 1st and 2nd bathroom, the master room, and common room 2. It measures about 23 ft wide and 22 ft long. The master room is at the north-west part of the building, to the right of common room 1, south of the balcony, and north  of the living room. It measures 14 ft wide and 17 ft long.
