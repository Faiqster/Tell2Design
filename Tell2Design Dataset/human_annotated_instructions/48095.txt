The balcony is at the southeast of the building, and it is behind the master room. It measures about 15ft wide and 3ft long. The bathroom is at the west of the house, and adjoins the living room. It measures about 4ft wide and 5ft long. The common room  1 is at the north part of the building. It adjoins the kitchen and living room. It measures about 12ft wide and 13ft long. Common room 2 is at the southwest of the building, and it is behind the living room. It  measures about 12 ft wide and 13ft long. The kitchen is in the north of the building and right next to common room 1. It measures about 5ft wide and 8ft long. The living room is in the middle of the house and behind the kitchen and common room 1. It measures about 20ft wide and 12ft long. The master room is on the east of the building and is behind the living room and right next to living room 2. It measures about 12ft wide and 14ft long.
