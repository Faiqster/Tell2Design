Balcony 1 is at the south-east part of the building and measures about 10ft wide and 3ft long. Balcony 2 is at the south of the building and adjacent to balcony 1 and measures about 9ft wide and 3 ft long. The bathroom is at the east of the building and adjacent to the living room. It measures about 5ft wide and 7ft long. Common room 1 is at the north-east part of the building, and bordering the living room to the north. It measures 12ft wide and 12ft long. Common room 2 is at the south-west part of the building, bordering the living room to the east. It measures about 10 ft wide and 14ft long. The kitchen is at the north-west part of the building and opposite common room 1. It measures about 6ft wide and 11ft long. The living room is at the center of the house, and all the other rooms bordering it. it measures about 15 ft wide and 20 ft long. The master room is at the south-east part of the building, to the east of the living room, and between balcony 1 and the bathroom. It measures about 12 ft in width and 14ft in length.
