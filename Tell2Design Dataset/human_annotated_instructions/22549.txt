Balcony 1 is on the south side, south of both the common room and the living room. It is about 20 feet across and 4 feet deep. Balcony 2 is on the west side, south of the master and west of the living room. It is about 5 feet across and 4 feet deep. The bathroom is on on east side in the middle, between the common room and the kitchen. It is about 6 feet wide and 9 feet long. The common room is on the east side, north of balcony 1 and south of the bathroom. It is about 9 feet wide and 14 feet long. The kitchen is in the northeast corner, north of the bathroom and across the living room from the master. It is about 5 feet square. The living room is in the middle of the floorplan, with balcony 1 to the south, the master and balcony 2 to the west, and the rest of the rooms to the east. It is about 30 feet long and 15 feet wide. The master room is in the northeast corner, north of balcony 2 and across the living room from the kitchen and the bathroom. It is about 11 feet wide and 14 feet deep.
