Balcony is very small and is situated on the north western corner of the house. Bath room 1 is next to the master room. It is small and almost square in shape and next to bathroom 2. Bathroom 2  is in between common room 2 and bathroom 1. It is also the same measurement of bathroom 1. Common room 1 is situated on the south wester part of the house. It is rectangle in shape and connected to living room. Common room 2 is larger than common room 1 and almost square in shape. It is situated on the north eastern corner of the house. Kitchen is below the balcony. It is small and rectangle in shape. One side of the kitchen is connected with the living room. Living room occupies the center portion of the house. It is lengthy, rectangle and narrow. It starts on the top northern end and ends till the southern end of the house. It is connected to all the other rooms of the house. Master room is bigger than the common room1 and common room 2. It is next to the living room and the bath room.
